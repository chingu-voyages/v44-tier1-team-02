RULES PLAY

About Us

The brains behind this game consist of a UI/UX designer named Ahmed and developers by the names of Anshuman Singh, Nadya Florins and Gayle Cindy. We are happy to have worked together in order to bring this game-idea to life.

Our Purpose

We are enthusiastic game designers who made a humorous and instructive game for kids. Our intention was to develop an entertaining, interactive experience that would teach kids while they were having a good time. We think that having fun helps kids learn more effectively. Because of this, we developed a game that is entertaining to play in addition to teaching multiplicaction. Through engaging games and activities, it is our goal to encourage children's exploration, learning, and development.

Our Goal

In our ideal world, kids will have access to top-notch educational games that are made to be entertaining and interesting. We are dedicated to developing cutting-edge games that inspire and teach since we think they may be an effective instrument for learning and development.

What we stand for

Education: We work to make learning accessible and pleasant for everyone because we believe that education is a fundamental right for every child.

Fun: We think learning should be enjoyable. Kids may learn multiplication while having fun because our game is made to be interesting and enjoyable for them.

Innovation: We are dedicated about providing cutting-edge and successful learning experiences by utilizing the most recent technology and research.

Accessibility: We think all kids should have access to top-notch educational games, regardless of their circumstances or upbringing.




We appreciate you picking out our game. We hope your youngster will enjoy and learn from it for a number of hours.
